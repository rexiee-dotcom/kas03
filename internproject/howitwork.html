Steps to swap the battery

  1. Park the EV
    Park at the battery swapping station on a marked slot.
  2. Power Off the Vehicle
    Turn off the ignition and ensure the vehicle is in parking mode.
  3. Unlock Battery Compartment
    Use a physical latch, button, or touchscreen to open the battery bay.
  4. Remove the Old Battery
    A technician or robotic arm will disconnect and remove the depleted battery.
  5. Insert the Charged Battery
    A fully charged battery is slid or lifted into place and connected securely.
  6. Lock and Secure
    Confirm the battery is locked in place (manually or automatically).
  7. Power On and Check
    Turn on the vehicle and check the battery level on the dashboard.
  8. Complete Transaction (if needed)
    Pay or confirm the swap digitally if it's a paid service.
